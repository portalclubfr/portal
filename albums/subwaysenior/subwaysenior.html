This is my first album, ever!

This record includes some songs that ended up being very special to me and to others as well. Initially, I didn't take it too seriously. I had been working on music projects in FL Studio for a long time and had also composed some video game tracks for my personal projects, like Splat-Plat. I think you can tell it has a micropop/video game-inspired vibe.
